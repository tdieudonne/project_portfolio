Hackathon Project: VISION-X

ABOUT VISION-X

VISION-X is an innovative project designed to assist people with visual impairments, especially those who are blind. Our technology aims to enhance navigation and independence in daily life.

Key Features

  • Smart glasses with built-in navigation assistance
  • Real-time obstacle detection and warning system
  • Voice-guided instructions for safe navigation

Implementation Details

Users wear our specially designed glasses, which use advanced sensors and AI to analyze the surrounding environment. The system provides audio instructions to guide the user safely through their surroundings.

what to expect from the project

This project helps visually impaired individuals navigate the world around them, which is full of obstacles and unexpected challenges. HACKASON aims to provide greater independence and confidence in daily activities.

WHAT IS THE VISION-X MISSION

At VISION-X, our mission is to empower visually impaired individuals by providing them with cutting-edge technology that enhances their ability to navigate and interact with the world independently.
